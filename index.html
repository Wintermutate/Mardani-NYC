Our Gallery

Mardani Fine Minerals is a gallery space dedicated to exhibiting the finest minerals available. Visitors are invited to view, ask about and experience fine mineral specimens in a unique and contemporary New York gallery setting. See beautiful minerals classically displayed on pedestals and in showcases, and enjoy them in our living installation where you can imagine them in a home setting while you peruse the numerous reference materials we have available.

Our Custom Displays

We believe fine minerals are a form of natural art that can be beautiful complements to any space. From homes to offices, and everything in-between, minerals have found their way into modern design. For those that need assistance in placing their specimens, we offer installation and retrofitting services, scalable to any collection.
