From 'Mine' to Yours

Our minerals are expertly curated by fine mineral dealer, Daniel Trinchillo. Every specimen in our gallery is a reflection of his exacting standards, standards that have been honed and developed throughout his twenty-five-year career. Specimens at Mardani are selected based on several, classic collecting standards such as mineral rarity, color intensity, crystal perfection and historic localities, and must also adhere to his discriminating aesthetics.
