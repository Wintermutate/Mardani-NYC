Tourmaline, Quartz & Albite

Inventory #07773

Locality

Pederneira Mine, São José da Safira, Minas Gerais, Southeast Region, Brazil

Dimensions

25.5cm x 12.8cm x 12cm

Weight

1263g

  • Description

    The Pederneira Mine of Brazil is known for producing some of the most magnificent specimens of tourmaline ever recovered. This beautiful specimen is a taste of the spectacular colors, and diverse associated minerals produced from the mine. It exhibits the characteristic, elongated crystal form of Brazilian tourmaline with a bright pink cap on a sea-green body. At its base is a core of dark pink coloring, partially embedded in a matrix of albite and lepidolite. A complementary quartz flanks the tourmaline crystal for yet another point of visual interest. It was formerly a part of the Wayne Sorenson Collection.

  • Provenance

    Ex. Wayne Sorenson Collection
