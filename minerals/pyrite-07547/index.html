Pyrite

Inventory #07547

Locality

Navajún, La Rioja, Spain

Dimensions

15cm x 10cm x 8cm

  • Description

    Pyrites are known around the world for their stunning geometry and brilliant shine. This highly sculptural specimen is from Navajun (pronounced (Navahoon), Spain, one of the premier localities of for cubic pyrite… need to expand on this… cubic examples. Each pyrite is a completely natural formation, formed over 130 million years ago. Culturally it has long been a symbol of vitality, wealth and a talisman of protection. This example challenges what we believe to be naturally possible by exhibiting incredible balance and symmetry.
